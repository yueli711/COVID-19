##  Welch Two Sample t-test
## 
## data:  test_validation$Activity[c(11:13, 18:20)] and test_validation$Activity[c(14:16, 21:23)]
## t = -78.859, df = 5.0321, p-value = 5.616e-09
## alternative hypothesis: true difference in means is not equal to 0
## 95 percent confidence interval:
##  -0.9886264 -0.9263235
## sample estimates:
##    mean of x    mean of y 
## 0.0006864964 0.9581614246
